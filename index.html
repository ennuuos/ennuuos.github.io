About Me

Hi, I'm Travis.

I'm a developer from Sydney, New South Wales, who's been programming, designing websites and making games for the better part of 8 years.

I'm currently working full time as a Software Developer at Envisage Software Solutions, a software company in my home town that focuses mostly on integrations with and between business systems. I've also worked on contracts for CSIRO, relating to the ASKAP telescope.

I'm currently working on getting a Bachelor's degree in computer science and technology from the University of Sydney.

The language I use most most is Typescript (a superset of JavaScript). When making applications I prefer to use React, and have a few different projects which use it.

I've worked on software ranging from mobile apps to integrations with accounting systems to boilerplate code for telescope monitoring for engineers.
I prefer to create applications rather than integrations, because I enjoy designing UI and UX.

You can see some of my work on github, or contact me for information about specific projects related to your needs.
